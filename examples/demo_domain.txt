E-Commerce Customer Support Agency

Domain: Online retail customer support for a mid-size e-commerce platform.

Key functions:
- Handle customer inquiries about orders, shipping, returns, and refunds
- Process return and refund requests
- Escalate complex issues to human supervisors
- Track order status and provide updates
- Manage product information queries
- Handle billing and payment issues
- Collect customer feedback and satisfaction surveys

Integrations needed:
- Order management system
- Payment processing
- Shipping tracking APIs
- Customer database (CRM)
- Knowledge base for product info and policies
